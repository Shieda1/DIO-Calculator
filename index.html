What is the DIO calculator?
The DIO calculator is a tool that determines the time it takes for a company for inventory turnover into sales.
How to calculate DIO?
To calculate DIO you should use the following formula:
DIO = (((starting inventory + final inventory) / 2) / cost of goods sold) * days in accounting period
Why use DIO calculator?
The DIO calculator is used in order to calculate the time it takes for a company for inventory turnover into sales.
